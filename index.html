About Tanjiro Kamado

He is the older brother of Nezuko Kamado and is the oldest child of the Kamado family. He is a Kanoe ranked Demon Slayer and a member of the Demon Slayer Corps.

Tanjiro Kamado's Characteristics

  1. He is gentle to a fault.
  2. He is brave enough to fight a demon slayer unarmed.
  3. He is never afraid to ask others for help.
  4. He is caring and protective of his friends, especially when it comes to his sister Nezuko.
